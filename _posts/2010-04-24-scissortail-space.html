I’ve been volunteering at the Scissortail social space the last couple weeks, helping out with their bike shop- it’s all pay as you can/free. If you’re having bike problems, come by wednesday after 2, and I’ll be glad to help. I mean, look at that truing stand.
They also have a decent-sized lending library, lots of good books that I have a feeling are hard to find in the MLS libraries. So come hang out, have some coffee (if we have any) and fix yo’ bike. It’s just south of the corner of 30th and walker, right here, next to the AA Food Mart.
