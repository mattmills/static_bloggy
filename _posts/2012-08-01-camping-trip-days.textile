These are the day photos; let the recording angel not that this is the Illinois River, outside Talequah; That we spent 7 hours floating 6 miles; that by the end, I was burnt, exhausted, and took a two hour nap the moment we got back to camp. 

Recommended: take a waterproof parasol, a camera with a housing, and use a canoe. Canoes go faster.
