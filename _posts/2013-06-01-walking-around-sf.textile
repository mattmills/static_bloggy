So my walk to work from the Bart is mostly down 3rd st, which is always interesting. Good light, good sidewalks, always changing.

I'm not sure why I bother doing the whole street thing. There's a horde of amatures brute forcing the entire street photo-space (the set of possible photos you can take on the street). But the heart wants. _Le cœur a ses raisons que la raison ne connaît point_, yeah? (Pascal, natch).
