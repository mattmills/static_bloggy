--- layout: post title: broken social scene date: 2011-03-30 ---

went out to tulsa, got to see a really good show. Broken social sceneplayed. I’d heard BSS at parties, on the spy, etc, and a bunch of friends were going. So I went, not quite on a whim, but only with a couple days notice. Good show, good brunch the day after. I was sad that I didn’t even try to take my camera into the venue. A friend told me that they weren’t letting people with cameras in and so I didn’t try; turns out they didn’t care. And I could have been in the front row, too. Oh well.

Meanwhile, my photographic output has dropped from little to nothing. had a bad shoot with KJ and just haven’t shot a thing in the week and a half since. It’s nobody’s fault, really, that the shoot went badly. I should have seen that what were doing wouldn’t work, though. It’s like I need to completely start from scratch and learn to shoot pictures all over again, and forget all the things I’ve learned. Throw away 13 years of experience and start fresh. Or maybe it’s a change of scene that I need; I don’t know. I’ll figure something out.
